# 🚚 Delivery Performance & Customer Experience Analytics Dashboard

## 📖 About the Project

As online delivery services continue to grow, maintaining both speed and customer satisfaction has become increasingly important. In this project, I built a **Power BI dashboard** to analyze delivery performance and customer experience across **10 cities**.

The goal was to understand not just *how fast* deliveries were made, but also *how reliable* the service was and how different operational issues affected customer satisfaction and retention. By bringing all the important KPIs into one interactive dashboard, I was able to uncover key business insights and suggest practical improvements.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Although deliveries were being completed quickly, customer complaints and churn remained high. This project focuses on answering questions such as:

* Are fast deliveries leading to better customer satisfaction?
* What are the biggest reasons behind customer complaints?
* Which cities require operational improvements?
* What actions can help improve customer retention?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

The dashboard includes:

* Revenue and order performance overview
* Delivery performance tracking
* Customer satisfaction (CSAT) analysis
* Complaint analysis
* Customer churn analysis
* City-wise performance comparison
* Interactive filters for deeper analysis

---

## 📈 Key Findings

* The business generated **₹951K+** in revenue across **10 cities**.
* The average delivery time was **17 minutes**, showing that deliveries were generally fast.
* However, only **42.5%** of orders were delivered on time, highlighting a reliability issue.
* **26.77%** of customers raised complaints, with app issues, missing items, and SLA breaches being the most common reasons.
* Customer churn reached **66.27%**, suggesting that service consistency plays a major role in customer loyalty.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

Based on the analysis, I suggested a few practical improvements:

* Provide more accurate and transparent delivery ETAs.
* Introduce quality checks before dispatch to reduce missing items.
* Improve app stability to minimize customer frustration.
* Focus retention efforts on customers most likely to churn.
* Track city-level KPIs regularly to identify operational gaps early.
